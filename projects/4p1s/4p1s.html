4 Players 1 Snake
Date of delivery: March 2016
Client: Samsung SRT
Tools used:
Unity3D
Description
4 Players 1 Snake is a unity project for Samsung Smart TVs. It works by running on the smart tv and then players connect to the application by running an Unity application on their smartphones. Then the controls for snake get divided by players so each player has a direction and they have to work together to control the snake.
